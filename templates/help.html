Help & FAQ

Frequently asked questions and usage guide

How to Use

1. How do I download a YouTube video?

Simply copy and paste the YouTube video URL into the input field on the homepage and click "Fetch Video". You'll then be presented with various resolution options to choose from.

2. How do I download audio only?

After fetching the video, you'll see audio-only options in the resolution list. These are labeled as "Audio" and show the bitrate (e.g., 128kbps). Select one of these options to download just the audio.

3. What video formats are supported?

The downloader supports MP4 video format. For audio-only downloads, you can choose from various quality options ranging from 64kbps to 320kbps.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I download some videos?

Some YouTube videos have restrictions that prevent downloading. This includes:

  • Age-restricted content
  • Copyright-protected material
  • Live streams (before or during broadcast)
  • Videos with special regional restrictions

Download failed or interrupted?

If a download fails or gets interrupted, try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try downloading a different resolution
  3. Refresh the page and try again
  4. Clear your browser cache

Why is my download slow?

Download speeds depend on several factors:

  • Your internet connection speed
  • YouTube's server load
  • Video resolution (higher = slower)
  • Current traffic on the network
The progress bar will show real-time download status.

Legal & Ethical Usage

Is this legal?

This tool is legal to use, but you should:

  • Only download content you have rights to
  • Respect content creators' intellectual property
  • Use downloaded content for personal purposes only
  • Comply with YouTube's Terms of Service

What about copyright?

Copyright is an important consideration. We recommend:

  • Only downloading videos you created or own
  • Getting permission from content creators before downloading
  • Not redistributing downloaded content without permission
  • Checking if content has a Creative Commons license
